Welcome to the Travel Area!

I've always loved to travel. I've been to 7 other countries, not including the US, and I have found myself wanting to go back to each and every one of them. Even if it means to visit another state in the US, I'll take the chance to see it. My next destination is Hawaii; I haven't been there before and I would really like to see what paradise looks like. I alwasy tend to take pictures whenever I travel, and coming back home with memories and once in a life time experiences are honestly the best part. Being able to learn about how life is like in another perspective is interesting and can make me grow as a person.
