Welcome to the Food Area!


On the right, are some of my favorite kinds of food to eat. When it comes to food, I'm not that picky, and I usually tend to mix and match, for say, "play with my food" when I really enjoy one type of dish. If I am used to it, I might combine it with another dish, and sometimes craete my own new dish with a little bit of immagination and creativity. When it comes to dessert, if I come to dessert, I tend to not be heavy with it and try to get something simple like ice-cream, or a hot fudge brownie. Taking something simple and transforming it into something different can satisfy, just about anyone. The best way to have dessert, in my opinion, is to make it at home. Home-made dessert can be simple to make, and can make you feel accomplished and satisfied at the same time. It's up to you to create your dish; you are the painter behind the canvas.It might take some time, but in the end, it will be all worth it. :)
